Tiger

  • Strong leadership that is usually financially-driven to deliver high margins from lean operations.
  • Tigers are potentially vulnerable to paradigm shifts in their industry, such as from entrants with disruptive technologies or business models.
Tiger Recommendations
  1. Consider disrupting your own business before someone else does it!
  2. Prepare for market change: consolidation and lean operations do not constitute a growth strategy.
  3. Create key performance metrics that are not cost and capability based.
  4. Encourage management to sponsor innovation programs that include exploration of new business models.
