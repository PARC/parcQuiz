Dolphin
Intelligent and highly social mammals that stay curious. Dolphins have relatively mature innovation culture and strategy.
  • There is clear management support for innovation.
  • The innovation agenda is more or less aligned with business strategies across the company.
  • Supporting innovation mechanisms (processes, tools, coaching, funds…) are well deployed.
  • Everyone, or almost everyone, can get trained, supported, and staffed on innovation projects.
Dolphin Recommendations
  1. You are ready for advanced strategic management of innovation:
    1. Consider establishing a cross-company coordination function for innovation to ensure consistent access to best practice process, tools, and coaching.
    2. Manage your innovation portfolio by consolidating innovation initiatives for better resource utilization.
    3. Link innovation activities explicitly to growth strategy to ensure the highest ROI.
  2. Explore both inbound (ideas, in-licensing) and outbound (out-licensing, spin outs) Open Innovation with partners whose capabilities complement yours.
  3. Improve/develop ways of evaluating innovation programs.
  4. Beware of strategy decay: Today’s leaders are vulnerable to the belief that their markets and business models will always be secure.
